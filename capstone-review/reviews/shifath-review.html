Design Review

Easy to Find the Solution: 8/10 - Both the projects were easy to find the solution.

Self-Explanatory: 8/10 - Both of them are self-explanatory.

Usability of the Site: 7.5/10 - Minimal design for the research paper site and good design for the weather app.

Pleasing Aesthetics: 7.5/10 - The weather app has simple and neat visuals. The research paper site could use more color themes for visual appeal.

Code Review

Code Readability: 8/10 - The code is well-written for all projects.

Code Organization: 8/10 - Good organization in folder structure across all projects.

Developer Review

Ease of Design Change: 7/10 - The research paper app has scope for improved design.

Ease of Functionality Change: 8/10 - The filter functionality is good, and fetching weather data works well.

Scalability: 7.5/10 - Both projects demonstrate reasonable scalability.

Final Comments

Overall Rating: 7.5/10

Good efforts overall. Enhancing the visual appeal and design of the research paper app will further improve the user experience.
