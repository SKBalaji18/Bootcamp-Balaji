Level 1: Recreating a Simple Webpage Layout

For Level 1, I recreated the layout of a simple webpage, such as a Medium article. This involved ensuring consistent margins and padding across sections to achieve a polished and balanced look. White space was used strategically to create clear separation between sections, enhancing readability and reducing visual clutter.

Aligning text and images correctly was a key focus, as improper alignment can make the layout feel disorganized. The challenge was maintaining consistency in spacing while adapting the layout to different screen sizes.
