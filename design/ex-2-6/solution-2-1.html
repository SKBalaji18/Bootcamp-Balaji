1. Indian Government Press Releases

Problems:
  • Unintuitive navigation makes it hard to find recent press releases.
  • Design lacks a modern, responsive interface.
  • Minimal filtering options for sorting by categories or dates.
Strengths:
  • Comprehensive archive of press releases.
  • Information is official and credible.
Inspiration:
  • US Department of State Press Releases:
    • Modern and clean interface.
    • Advanced filtering by topic and region.
    • Clearly categorized and visually appealing.

2. Single Press Release

Problems:
  • Overloaded with text, making it visually unappealing.
  • Lack of a summary or highlights section.
  • No shareable media or links to related press releases.
Strengths:
  • Provides detailed information on the subject.
  • Official tone ensures credibility.
Inspiration:
  • Newsroom articles from Apple or Microsoft:
    • Well-structured with bullet points and visual media.
    • Clear and concise summaries.
    • Relevant links to previous announcements.

3. IITM CSE Faculty Site

Problems:
  • Outdated design that lacks responsiveness.
  • Faculty profiles are inconsistently formatted.
  • Minimal interactivity, such as search or filtering.
Strengths:
  • Provides essential academic and contact information.
  • Includes links to publications and research areas.
Inspiration:
  • Stanford CS Faculty Page:
    • Clean, modern interface with a search bar.
    • Consistent formatting across profiles.
    • Integrated multimedia and quick links.

4. Single Faculty Member

Problems:
  • Profile lacks visual appeal and readability.
  • No clear hierarchy of information (e.g., publications vs. teaching).
  • Minimal links to external academic work or projects.
Strengths:
  • Provides necessary academic background and achievements.
Inspiration:
  • MIT Faculty Profiles:
    • Clear, attractive layout.
    • Prominent links to ongoing projects and labs.
    • Integrated media such as videos or talks.

5. IIT B Talk

Problems:
  • Poor categorization of past talks and events.
  • Lack of an intuitive calendar or event reminder feature.
  • No visual appeal for event descriptions.
Strengths:
  • Comprehensive details about talks and events.
Inspiration:
  • TED Talk Website:
    • Visually appealing with speaker photos and highlights.
    • Clear categorization and recommendation system.
    • Well-integrated with multimedia content.

6. Indian Railways Website

Problems:
  • Cluttered design with overwhelming information on the homepage.
  • Slow loading times and outdated visual design.
  • Complex navigation for ticket booking and status checking.
Strengths:
  • Provides comprehensive services for ticket booking, cancellations, and inquiries.
Inspiration:
  • Trainline (UK):
    • Simple, user-friendly interface.
    • Fast and efficient ticket booking process.
    • Real-time updates on train status and schedules.
