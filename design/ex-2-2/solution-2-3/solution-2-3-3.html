Accessibility Guidelines

  • WCAG 2.0 Level AA requires a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text
  • Large text (14pt and bold or 18pt and regular) requires a minimum contrast ratio of 3:1
  • Use color contrast checker tools to verify accessibility
